Daniel Donaldson

I want craft with excellence.

Two years ago I decided to make a change. I left a career in the non-profit field to pusue web design. I was convinced I could make a wider impact on the world via web applications.

Web applications are about changing behavior, being a catalyst of decision. In my opinion, the harmony betwee design and development help to craft better solutions. I'm looking for a position on a design team as a front-end developer where I can be part of that process.

Experience

I've freelanced for a couple of years and worked with some great people. I was the UX Intern for a great web shop in Dallas. I was later promoted to Project Coordinator, but I left there to attend The Iron Yard Academy.

I also have a degree in psychology, which I use just about every day when I code & collaborate.
